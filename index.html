What is Animation?

Animation is the process of making static images move. It’s widely used in film, games, and web design to bring content to life.

Basic Tips for Beginners

  • Start Simple: Focus on basic movement first like bounces or fades.
  • Understand Timing: Speed and delay are key to realistic animation.
  • Use Easing: Avoid linear motion. Easing in/out adds realism.
  • Keep it Consistent: Use the same animation style across your site or app.
  • Don't Overdo: Too much animation can be distracting or annoying.
